Jujutsi Kaisen

Genres - Animation, Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Thriller

Yuji Itadori, a kind-hearted teenager, joins his school's Occult Club for fun, but discovers that its members are actual sorcerers who can manipulate the energy between beings for their own use. He hears about a cursed talisman - the finger of Sukuna, a demon - and its being targeted by other cursed beings.

Yuji eats the finger to protect his friends, and ends up becoming Sukuna's host.

If you get asteroids about a kilometer in size, those are large enough and carry enough energy into our system to disrupt transportation, communication, the food chains, and that can be a really bad day on Earth.

However, Yuji discovers that he has inherited magic and is able to control this power without interference from Sukuna.

He joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College to consumes all of Sukuna's fingers, which will enable a full exorcism to take place that will free him.
